City teams

Five people in every city. A president, a vice president, and three directors.

  • Chapter president

    Leads the chapter. Represents it internationally. Sets goals. Oversees all activity in the city.

  • Vice president

    Supports the president. Coordinates projects and internal operations. Steps in when the president is unavailable.

  • Director of research

    Leads research projects. Coordinates publications and technical work.

  • Director of events

    Plans and runs the chapter's workshops and events.

  • Social media manager

    Communications. Runs the chapter's channels.
